Smart Property Showcase

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Smart Property Showcase is a modern real estate platform. The name emphasizes a tech-forward approach to showcasing curated and smart property listings.

Suggested Domain: smartpropertyshowcase.com

Site Purpose

The website will allow users to search, browse, and schedule visits to listed properties. It will also offer smart filtering tools, virtual tours, and real-time market insights.

User Scenarios

  • “What smart homes are available within my ₦80M budget in Lekki?”
  • “How can I schedule a video tour for this property?”

Color Scheme

  • Primary Sky Blue: #74b9ff – Used in headers, buttons, and accents.
  • Neutral Background: #f5fafd – Used for background and content sections.

Typography

  • Inter: For headers and titles
  • Open Sans: For body text
