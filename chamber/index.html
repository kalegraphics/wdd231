What We Do

  • Support local businesses
  • Promote economic growth
  • Connect entrepreneurs
  • Advocate for the community

Founded with the vision of building a stronger, more connected business community, the Timbuktu Chamber of Commerce brings together local businesses, nonprofits, and civic leaders. We advocate for policies that benefit our members, provide resources to help businesses grow, and organize events that promote networking and professional development. Our organization is built on the values of integrity, inclusiveness, innovation, and collaboration.
